The making of IanBT

IanBT is a singer based out of Brooklyn, New York, currently attending the Hartt School of Music. Credits include The King in Laitman’s The Three Feathers, Le Chat in Faure’s L’Enfant et le Sortileges, and Jean Valjean in Les Misérables. In addition to performing classical works, Ian writes and produces his own music, which can be found on all major streaming platforms under “IanBT”.
